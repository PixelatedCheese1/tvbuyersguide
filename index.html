The Ultimate TV Buyers Guide
Any TV to run Google TV (TCL, Sony or Hisense TVs found to be the best) - Google
TV runs Android meaning that you will have a lot of freedom. Such as sideloading,
installing custom homes and many many more! Great app catalog, has every app that
you can think of, and is updated faster than you can say chipmunk. Supports
Chromecast meaning you can easily connect your chromecast connected devices such
as: Phones, Chromebooks and even VR headsets! Comes with amazing Google
Gemini. The most powerfullest AI out there to give you great recommendations. Also
includes amazing soundbar support. Rating 9.6/10
High powered Roku Devices - Roku has a great app catalog but not the best. Users
found that Roku devices are super speedy along with many bugs. Voice assistant is
trash, they never find anything you need. Has many ADs especially with the new home
screen update. Roku updates 5 times a year, which isn't great. Doesnt support
Chromecast. Meaning you can really only connect your phone and that is about it. All
Roku devices dont have a browser and most apps have to be built off the Roku
Framework. Meaning there will be many bugs in apps that dont have that much
support. Rating 7/10
Samsung - Samsung uses TizenOS, which many people absolutely hate! Samsung
is quick and snappy until you realize its riddled with ADs. Users find it very hard to
navigate since of the ADs. The app catalog isnt great either due to low demand for
Samsung TVs. Very underpowered voice assistant. See [page] for more details.
Rating 1.5/10
Vizio - Vizio TVs use SmartCastOS. People say it's worse than Samsungs TizenOS.
It's way more riddled with ADs. If you use this TV just get an external device.
Rating 0.2/10
AppleTVOS - Only used on external Apple TV boxes. Useful if you have a TV like
Vizio. Its sleek, clean and minimal. Rating 8/10
LG WebOS - WebOS is okay. There isn't a very good app library and it was built from a
failed smartphone running WebOS, so in that case it isn't very reliable. It doesn't have
as many bugs as Roku and apps have to be built using an internet browsers
framework. WebOS mostly forces Microsofts Copilot on you and its not very good.
Rating 6/10
What to avoid, when buying a TV
You should always avoid buying TVs like onn, Element, Westinghouse, Philips, Insignia,
RCA, Toshiba, Vizio and Samsung.
